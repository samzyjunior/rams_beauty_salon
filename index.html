About Ruby

Ruby's Story

Ruby's passion for makeup began in her childhood, where she would experiment with her mom's cosmetics and create looks for her friends and family. As she grew older, her interest only deepened, leading her to pursue professional training in makeup artistry.

With years of experience and a keen eye for detail, Ruby has established herself as a sought-after makeup artist in Madina, Ghana. Her services include bridal makeup, party makeup, and special occasion makeup.
